SELECTION STEPS & REGULATIONS
01
Report to the SSB centre on the given date with all original documents, including 10th marksheet, graduation certificate, and valid ID proof.
02
Day 1 consists of Screening: OIR Test (verbal + non-verbal intelligence) and PPDT (Picture Perception & Discussion Test).
03
Only candidates who clear Stage 1 Screening proceed to Stage 2 (Psychology Tests on Day 2).
04
Psychology tests include TAT (Thematic Apperception Test), WAT (Word Association Test), SRT (Situation Reaction Test), and SD (Self Description).
05
GTO tasks are conducted over 2 days and include Group Discussion, Group Planning Exercise, PGT, HGT, IOGAT, Command Task, and Lecturette.
06
Personal interview is scheduled by the Interviewing Officer (IO) and is one-on-one. No specific format — be genuine and factual.
07
Conference is the final stage where the Board collectively evaluates your performance across all previous stages.
08
Medical examination is conducted after recommendation. Final merit is based on written exam + SSB score combined.

Who We Are
ABOUT SSB ASPIRANT
We are a dedicated defence education platform built by ex-servicemen and SSB-qualified mentors to prepare the next generation of Indian Officers.

Our Mission

To democratise SSB preparation by providing every aspirant — regardless of geography or economic background — access to world-class simulation tools, mock tests, and expert guidance.

We believe every deserving candidate who dreams of serving the nation must have a fair shot at the Services Selection Board.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted digital SSB coaching platform, recognised by the Armed Forces for producing confident, well-prepared officer candidates.

We envision a future where digital preparation bridges the gap between aspirants and selection, making India's officer cadre stronger and more diverse.

Our Story

Founded in 2021 by a group of Indian Army officers who cleared the SSB themselves, our platform was born out of a simple frustration: quality SSB preparation was expensive, geographically limited, and often outdated.

We built SSB Aspirant Portal to solve exactly that — a digital, always-on simulation platform that mirrors the real board experience.

Our Approach

We don't teach shortcuts. We focus on building genuine Officer Like Qualities — leadership, initiative, communication, and courage under pressure — through immersive simulation and structured feedback.

Every feature in our portal is designed around what the actual SSB assessors look for, not textbook theory.
